# Email Sequences

> 3-step outreach sequence for the Q3-2026-Primary-Outreach campaign.
> Variables wrapped in `{{double_braces}}` are auto-populated from Clay enrichment data.

---

## Sequence Overview

| Step | Timing | Purpose | Expected Reply Rate |
|------|--------|---------|-------------------|
| Email 1 | Day 0 | Initial cold outreach | 2-4% |
| Email 2 | Day 3 | Soft follow-up with social proof | 1-3% |
| Email 3 | Day 7 | Breakup / final attempt | 1-2% |

**Total expected reply rate**: 4-9% (industry average for personalized B2B cold email is 3-8%)

- `{{first_name}} — noticed something about {{company}}`
- `{{pain_point_topic}} at {{company}}`

### Body

```

{{ai_personalized_email_body}}

```

> **Note:** The `{{ai_personalized_email_body}}` is generated by GPT in n8n Workflow 02 using the `outreach-personalizer.md` prompt. If GPT fails, fall back to the template below.

{{sender_name}}

## Email 2 — Follow-up with Social Proof (Day 3)

### Subject Line
*(Same thread — no new subject line, uses "Re:" automatically)*

### Body

```
Hi {{first_name}},

Wanted to follow up — {{pain_point_topic}} tends to be a recurring challenge for {{industry}} teams around your size.

We recently helped a similar company {{social_proof_result}}.

Would it be worth a quick 15-minute conversation to see if we can help {{company}} achieve something similar?

{{sender_name}}
```

### Variable Notes
- `{{social_proof_result}}`: Replace with your best case study result (e.g., "reduce onboarding time by 60%", "increase conversion rate by 3x")
- Keep this specific and quantified — vague claims like "improve efficiency" don't work

---

## Email 3 — Breakup Email (Day 7)

### Subject Line
*(Same thread — no new subject line)*

### Body

```
Hi {{first_name}},

I'll keep this short — if {{pain_point_topic}} isn't a priority for {{company}} right now, totally understand.

But if it is, I'd be happy to share what's worked for similar {{industry}} companies. Just reply "interested" and I'll send a quick overview.

Either way, no hard feelings.

{{sender_name}}
```

### Why This Works
- **Low pressure**: Gives them permission to say no
- **Simple CTA**: One-word reply lowers friction dramatically
- **Breakup psychology**: Creates subtle urgency without being pushy

## A/B Testing Strategy

| Element | Variant A | Variant B | Winner Criteria |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Subject Line | Question format | Statement format | Higher open rate after 50 sends |
| CTA | "15-minute call" | "quick chat" | Higher reply rate |
| Email 1 Length | GPT-generated (75 words) | Template (50 words) | Higher reply rate |

---

## Anti-Spam Best Practices Enforced

- ✅ No HTML or rich formatting
- ✅ No tracking links (open tracking only via custom domain)
- ✅ No images or attachments
- ✅ No more than 1 link per email (and even that is optional)
- ✅ Unsubscribe compliance via Instantly
- ✅ Physical address in signature (CAN-SPAM compliance)
- ✅ Daily limit capped at 30 (ramping from 10)
- ✅ 2-5 minute gaps between sends
- ✅ Weekend sends disabled
- ✅ Stop on reply enabled
